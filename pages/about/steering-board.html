Steering Board


The IRIS-HEP Steering Board represents the Institute’s stakeholders to provide, to the Executive Board, the stakeholder’s input on the priorities, execution, and strategy of the Institute.

Meetings

Meetings of the Steering Board take place approximately quarterly and can be found in the IRIS-HEP Steering Board Area in Indico. Meeting minutes are attached to each agenda.
